# Investment Principles

- Price is what you pay, value is what you get. Always compare price to
  intrinsic value before acting.
- Trend is your friend until it ends. Respect momentum but watch for
  divergence signals.
- Never average down into a deteriorating fundamental story.
- Position size is risk management. No single position should risk more than
  2% of portfolio equity.
- Macro trumps micro in regime shifts. When the Fed pivots, sector rotation
  matters more than individual stock picks.
- Gold and the dollar tend to move inversely; oil correlates with global
  growth expectations.
