# US Equity Deep-Dive SOP

1. **Company snapshot** — fetch latest price, market cap, sector, 52-week range
2. **Fundamentals** — P/E, P/S, EV/EBITDA, revenue growth, net margin, FCF yield
3. **Technicals** — trend (50/200 MA), RSI, volume pattern, key support/resistance
4. **Earnings** — last 4 quarters: beat/miss, guidance revision trend
5. **Catalysts** — upcoming earnings date, product launches, regulatory events
6. **Peer comparison** — how does valuation compare to sector peers?
7. **Recommendation** — Bull / Bear / Neutral with price target and stop-loss
8. **Risks** — list top 3 risks to the thesis
