# Macro Market Cycle Review SOP

1. **Fed policy stance** — current rate, dot plot, recent FOMC language
2. **Inflation picture** — CPI, PCE, PPI trends; is inflation rising or falling?
3. **Labor market** — NFP, unemployment rate, wage growth
4. **Growth indicators** — GDP, PMI (manufacturing + services), retail sales
5. **Yield curve** — 2s10s spread; inversion depth and duration
6. **Risk appetite** — VIX level, credit spreads (HY vs IG), equity breadth
7. **Sector rotation signal** — which sectors are outperforming in this regime?
8. **Overall regime call** — Risk-On / Risk-Off / Transitional
