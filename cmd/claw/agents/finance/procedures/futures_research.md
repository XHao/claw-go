# Commodities & Futures Research SOP

1. **Spot & futures price** — current price, front-month contract, contango/backwardation
2. **Supply/demand fundamentals** — inventory levels (EIA for oil/gas, CFTC COT for metals)
3. **Macro drivers** — USD index, real yields (for gold/silver), global growth proxies
4. **Seasonal patterns** — historical seasonal tendencies for the commodity
5. **Technicals** — trend, key levels, positioning (COT data)
6. **Geopolitical / event risk** — OPEC decisions, Fed meetings, sanctions
7. **Recommendation** — Long / Short / Flat with entry zone and stop
8. **Risks** — list top 3 risks
